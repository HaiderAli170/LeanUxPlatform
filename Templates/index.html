About Us

LEAN UX PLATFORM

Research History

Jamil Hussain and others, represents a significant milestone in the field of user experience research and evaluation.The research focuses on developing a platform that captures and analyzes various modalities of user data, including eye-tracking data, facial expressions, and other relevant metrics to evaluate and enhance the user experience effectively.

Vision Statement

Lean UX platform is to revolutionize the way user experiences are crafted, evaluated, and optimized, leveraging the power of eye and facial data. By integrating advanced eye-tracking and facial analysis techniques, the platform aims to provide unparalleled insights into user emotions, preferences, and engagement, ultimately creating captivating experiences that leave a lasting impact.

Introduction

Lean UX platform is user experience (UX) evaluation platform that provides physiological measurements for UX evaluation instead of traditional ones. By incorporating these techniques into UX research, designers can gain a deeper understanding of users' needs, emotions, and behavior to create products that are more engaging, satisfying, and effective

Our Goal

Our goal is to decode the language of the eyes and facial expressions, satisfaction, and emotional responses. With this data-driven approach, we strive to personalize experiences, tailoring them to individual users and fostering greater engagement.Our ultimate goal is to empower designers to create captivating, engaging, and personalized experiences that delight users and drive business success.
